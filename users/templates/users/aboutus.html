EXAM AUTOMATION SYSTEM

ABOUT US

It is a Web based application intended to automate all the work related to exam cell,which includes storing of midterms marks, practical marks(both external and internal) printing consolidated reports etc. Admin is responsible to create, department and to register faculty members and students to departments. He/She is also responsible to register subjects and laboratories running in different semester and to map students and faculties to appropriate subjects and labs. Faculty members are required to login into the system and fill midterms,practical marks into the system. admin can generate different type of consolidated reports.
